Spiderman: No Way Home

"Another spiderman that delivers a feel-good experience! From the cinematography to the casting of all 3 spidermen including Tobey Maguire, Andrew Garfield, and the latest Tom Holland, there is a lot packed into this 2.5 hour movie. The premise is that monsters and spiderman from multiple universes have all appeared in Tom Holland's reality, and with the help of Dr. Strange, played by Benedict Cumberbatch, he has to repair and return each one to its original universe. This proves to be no simple feat..."
